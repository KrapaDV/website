Glaze MIST is a specialized vertical of the Manipal Information Security Team (MIST), dedicated to exploring the intersection of creativity and cybersecurity. It blends the art of design with the science of infosec, offering a platform where visual storytelling meets technical brilliance. Glaze MIST focuses on UI/UX design, web aesthetics, branding, and graphical content that helps communicate complex cyber concepts in simple, engaging ways. This initiative not only supports MIST’s technical teams with visually impactful resources but also provides a space for designers and creative thinkers to thrive in the tech community.

More than just an artistic wing, Glaze MIST plays a crucial role in shaping MIST’s identity and outreach. From building user‑friendly interfaces for MIST’s internal tools to designing event posters, infographics, and social media content, Glaze ensures that the team’s work looks as sharp as it functions. It also organizes workshops, challenges, and design jams to promote visual literacy among technically inclined students, encouraging cross‑functional collaboration. Glaze MIST is where cybersecurity meets design thinking—fostering innovation through aesthetics and accessibility.
